Meet Our Team

Persis Boateng

Position: Founder & CEO

Persis Boateng is the visionary founder and CEO of Zikora STEM Society. With a passion for STEM education and a commitment to fostering inclusivity, Persis founded the organization in 2020. Her leadership has driven the society's mission to bridge educational gaps and provide quality STEM learning experiences to underserved communities.

Jemima Osei Kusi

Position: Co-Founder & Program Coordinator

As the Co-Founder and Program Coordinator, Jemima plays a pivotal role in leading Zikora STEM Society's educational programs. Although she is currently at Cornell University, Jemima still coordinates initiatives that bring hands-on learning experiences to students. Her dedication to empowering the next generation of STEM enthusiasts is evident through her work in developing and implementing impactful programs.
