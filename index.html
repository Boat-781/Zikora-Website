About Us

Zikora STEM Society is a non-profit organization driven by a passionate belief in the transformative power of STEM education. Founded in 2020, we are dedicated to breaking down barriers and empowering underserved communities by providing access to high-quality learning experiences in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our core pillars rest on mentorship, accessibility, and innovation. With a team of 15 dedicated individuals, including educators, and volunteers, we've impacted the lives of over 1,000 students through a blend of online and in-person workshops. These workshops impart knowledge and equip students with the tools and resources necessary to explore coding, research, and various STEM disciplines. At Zikora STEM Society, we're not just educators; we're mentors, advocates, and catalysts for change. Together, we strive to create a world where STEM education knows no bounds and where every aspiring mind has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Projects

Discover Zikora STEM Society's transformative STEM education initiatives, empowering and inspiring diverse communities through curiosity, equitable opportunities, and a passion for STEM.

Coding Bootcamps

A month-long intensive coding bootcamp designed for high school students in underserved areas. This initiative provided hands-on training in programming languages such as Python and JavaScript. Through partnerships with local tech companies, participants engaged in real-world projects, fostering practical skills and mentorship opportunities. At the conclusion, students showcased their projects at a community expo, demonstrating their newfound coding abilities.

STEM on Wheels

A mobile STEM lab initiative that brought interactive science and technology experiments directly to rural communities lacking access to traditional educational resources. The initiative equipped a bus with state-of-the-art STEM equipment and facilitated engaging workshops on robotics, circuitry, and scientific exploration. Trained instructors guided participants through immersive learning experiences, sparking curiosity and interest in STEM fields

STEM-preneur Workshops

Entrepreneurship-focused workshops aimed at fostering innovation and business acumen among aspiring young STEM enthusiasts. These workshops covered topics such as product ideation, market research, and prototype development. Participants collaborated in teams to create innovative solutions to societal challenges using STEM principles. The initiative concluded with a pitch competition, offering seed funding and mentorship to winning teams to further develop their ideas.

Materials for Learning

Robotics Kits

These kits include components like microcontrollers, sensors, motors, and building blocks that allow students to learn programming, engineering, and problem-solving through hands-on robotics projects. They enable practical application of STEM principles and foster creativity.

Coding Platforms and Software

Utilizing platforms like Scratch, Python, or Blocky, students learn coding fundamentals and computational thinking. Interactive software and online coding environments provide a user-friendly interface for beginners and advanced tools for more experienced learners.

Scientific Equipment and Kits

Access to scientific equipment and kits for experiments in physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science is crucial. These include microscopes, chemistry sets, electronic measurement tools, and environmental testing kits, enabling students to conduct experiments and explore scientific concepts firsthand.

Prototyping Tools

Innovation in STEM often involves prototyping and design. 3D printers and prototyping tools allow students to bring their ideas to life, promoting creativity and problem-solving skills while exploring engineering and design principles.

Accessible Textbooks and Reference Materials

Providing textbooks, reference materials, and reading resources ensures that students have access to foundational knowledge and in-depth information related to STEM subjects.
